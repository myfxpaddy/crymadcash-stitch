# Crymad Cash — Stitch Design Project

## What This Project Is
A fintech digital banking dashboard platform called "Crymad Cash" — designed with Google Stitch and converted to production React. The design should be fantasy-futuristic, glass morphism, living animated backgrounds, NOT generic AI/corporate slop.

## Design System
- Dark mode: Emerald Pulse — bg #040d0a, primary #10b981, secondary #34d399, accent #f59e0b
- Light mode: Arctic Light — bg #f0f5fa, primary #0d9488, secondary #2563eb
- Glass morphism: backdrop-blur, subtle borders rgba(primary, 0.08)
- Living background: organic blobs morphing, floating particles, energy streams
- Font: Inter (body) + JetBrains Mono (data/numbers)
- Animations: breathing SVG rings, flowing connection lines, pulse dots, shimmer effects
